Events

Stepple Race

A steeplechase race is a running race with barriers and water jumps on the track. Runners must jump over these obstacles while racing to the finish line.

Hurdle

A hurdle race is a running race where athletes jump over small barriers called hurdles. The hurdles are spaced evenly along the track, and runners must clear each one without stopping.

Running Sprint

A sprint is a short running race where athletes run as fast as they can. It usually happens over short distances like 100 or 200 meters.

High Jump

High jump is a sport where athletes run and try to jump over a high bar. They must jump without knocking the bar down.

Long Jump

Long jump is a sport where athletes run fast and jump as far as they can into a sand pit. The distance they jump is measured from the take-off point to where they land.

Pole Vault

Pole vault is a sport where athletes use a long pole to help them jump over a high bar. They run with the pole, plant it in the ground, and use it to launch themselves into the air.

Javelin

Javelin is a sport where athletes throw a long, spear-like stick as far as they can. They run up to gain speed and then throw it into a field.

Discus Thrower

Discus throw is a sport where athletes throw a heavy, flat disc as far as they can. They spin in a circle to gain power before releasing the discus.

Shotput

Shot put is a sport where athletes throw a heavy metal ball as far as they can. They push the ball from their shoulder using strength and technique.
